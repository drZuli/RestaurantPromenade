Our Story

Founded in 2015 by Chef Marco Abazi, Restaurant Promenade brings authentic Mediterranean flavors to Zurich with a contemporary twist. Our dishes celebrate the rich culinary traditions of the Mediterranean coast while incorporating seasonal Swiss ingredients.

We pride ourselves on:

  • • Hand-selected ingredients from local producers
  • • An extensive wine list featuring regional specialties
  • • Impeccable service in an elegant atmosphere
